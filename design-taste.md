## Rating System

**10/10 — Exceptional:** Completely distinctive, memorable, sophisticated
**7–9/10 — Strong:** Clearly avoids generic patterns, thoughtful decisions
**4–6/10 — Developing:** Some generic elements remain, needs refinement
**1–3/10 — Generic:** Looks like every other product in its category
